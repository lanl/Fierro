About Us

Our Goal

Our team at LANL is focused on advancing materials science research by paving the way for massively parallelized topology optimization. We aim to contibute to the academic community through active research and contribute to the engineering community by quickly integrating novel algorithms into our Fierro application. We see Fierro not only as single topology optimizer, but as an ever growing toolbox for solving engineering problems.

Our Philosophy

Historically there has been a gulf of space between high-tech materials simulations codes and easy to software. In the past users have needed a PhD just to run the best code out there. We strive to bridge that gap and not only develop cutting edge code with the most advanced algorithms, but also put it in the hands of real engineers. We know not everyone is an expert in high performance computer science applications, so whether its by simplifying the installation, simplifying the application, or simplifying source code contribution, we strive to enable more users to participate.
